How to Compute

🎯 NPS Score (0-10)

Formula: %Promoters - %Detractors

Promoters: 9-10
Passives: 7-8
Detractors: 0-6

Ex: 40% - 20% = +20

⭐ 5-Star Rating

Shows average out of 5

Ex: (5+4+3)/3 = 4.0

⭐ 3-Star Rating

Shows average out of 3

Ex: (3+2+3)/3 = 2.7

✓✗ Yes/No

Shows Yes percentage

Ex: 7Y+3N = 70%
